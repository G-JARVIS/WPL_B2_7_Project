Frequently Asked Questions

PFM is a Personal Finance Manager designed to help you track your income, expenses, and budgets in one place. It provides detailed analytics and insights so you can make informed financial decisions.
Once logged in, you can create and manage multiple wallets through the My Wallet section. For example, you can manage separate wallets for different family members, each with its own balance and transaction history.
You can update your personal and financial preferences from the Settings page. Options include changing your password, setting your default currency, and defining spending limits.
We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. Additionally, you have options like Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) to further secure your account.
Notification frequency can be customized from the Settings page. You can choose to receive notifications in real-time, as daily summaries, or as weekly summaries.
